## 🎯 Trading Logic: Why "Structure Over Funding"?

You have a sharp eye: **Negative funding (-0.002%) does indeed mean many people are shorting, which is "usually" a good time for a short squeeze (Long).**

However, in a professional trading system, there is an unbreakable hierarchy:
👉 **Price Action (Structure) > Liquidity > Derivative Data (Funding/OI)**

1.  **Macro Structure (4H) is Weakening**:

When price crashes from $71k but OI reaches a daily high of **2.91M BTC**, it suggests large institutional capital is building massive short positions at the top. This heavy selling pressure causes the negative flip. Smarter capital has the muscle to push price below $69,000.

3.  **No "Sweep" = No Trade**:
    To weaponize negative funding for a squeeze, we need a trigger that "traps" the shorts. We need to see price **violently break the $69,155 support on high volume, then V-reversal back above within 15 mins (a Fakeout / Sweep)**. Only then is longing the negative funding a high-probability strike.
